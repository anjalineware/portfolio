Blockchain-Based Identity Verification in Financial Services

This research paper explores the integration of blockchain technology in identity verification processes within the financial services sector. It highlights the challenges of traditional methods, such as fraud and inefficiency, and proposes a blockchain-based solution that enhances security, transparency, and user control over personal data. The paper discusses the potential benefits, including reduced costs and improved customer experience, while also addressing regulatory and technical challenges. The findings suggest that blockchain can revolutionize identity verification in finance, paving the way for more secure and efficient transactions.

E-GOVGUARD (EGG): A Governament Infrastructure Using Blockchain

eGovGuard is a secure e-government solution that combines OCR and blockchain to protect critical public data. Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for user interaction, it extracts information from documents via OCR and stores it on a private blockchain to ensure immutability and security. Smart contracts enable fast, secure transactions, while the system ensures transparency, data integrity, and trust in government processes. Ideal for sectors like healthcare, finance, and supply chains, eGovGuard strengthens digital governance for both citizens and officials.
